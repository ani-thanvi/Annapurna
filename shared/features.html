Main features of Annapurna

Annapurna as name signifies "Goddess of food and nourishment" is a platform where we believe in giving to the society and to the needy so that no stomach is left empty. From our Channels of most hygienic cloud kitchens and NGOs we deliver foods who are currently fighting in these tough times.

1. Free Delivery For Covid Patients and Frontline Workers.

In this pandemic times we believe in giving to the society and to the needy. Thus we offers free delivery to all the covid patients and frontline workers, healthy and hygienic food from the cloud kitchens and Covid Relief Food NGOs.

2. Source of income to the Chefs with Annapurna Hands.

The pandemic is hard for lower middle class families. So to help them in these tough times, we offer an opportunity to host Cloud Kitchen Services through our platform to provide Nutrients filled home cooked at your doorstep and earn some expenses.

3. Take Away Ordering

The take away ordering is a win-win solution for restaurant owners and customers. Restaurant owners get paid in advance, they save lot of time as they don’t have to take manual orders over the phone which can even cause errors and confusion. Customers don’t have to wait for the restaurant to answer the phone or ask the pickup time. They can simply go on to the website, see the menu, and place the order for the time they'd like to pick their food.
